Built on Hard Work. Backed by Experience. Trusted Across West Michigan.

Finishline Excavation & Concrete is a locally owned West Michigan concrete contractor serving homeowners from Grand Rapids to Kalamazoo. We specialize in concrete driveways, patios, garage slabs, and foundation work built to handle Michigan’s freeze-thaw climate.

When you hire Finishline, you’re not just getting concrete poured — you’re getting proper site preparation, reinforced slabs, clean job sites, and work done right the first time.

Why West Michigan Homeowners Choose Finishline

  • Proper Base Preparation – We focus on grading and compaction to prevent cracking and shifting.
  • Reinforced Concrete – Built to withstand heavy loads and Michigan winters.
  • Clear Communication – You know the timeline and what to expect.
  • Clean Job Sites – We respect your property from start to finish.
  • Licensed & Insured – Your investment is protected.

Concrete Experience That Shows in the Finish

We bring years of hands-on experience in residential and light commercial concrete projects. From new construction foundations to replacement driveways and custom patios, every project is approached with precision and attention to detail.

We understand West Michigan soil conditions and how to prepare properly for long-term durability — because a strong concrete slab starts below the surface.
