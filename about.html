About Finishline Excavation & Concrete

Finishline is a West Michigan excavation and concrete contractor serving homeowners and light commercial clients from Grand Rapids to Kalamazoo. We specialize in precision groundwork and durable concrete installations built to last.

Our Experience

We bring years of hands-on excavation and concrete experience to every project. From residential site preparation to concrete driveways and foundations, our team ensures quality workmanship and reliable timelines.

Equipment & Capability

Our modern excavation equipment allows us to handle grading, foundation digging, drainage systems, and structural concrete work efficiently and safely.

Safety & Professionalism

We operate with strict safety standards, maintain clean job sites, and are fully licensed and insured. Customer satisfaction and long-term durability are our priorities.
